My projects:

  • Driver Ultimate

    Drive and drift, complete daily quests and enjoy the game! This is a small car simulator with different game modes and 6 different cars.

  • JustNotes

    Simple and fast note taking app with Monet Engine support (Android 12+). There is a character counter support for creating a .txt file from a note. Focused on speed and design. Written in Kotlin.

  • JustWeather

    JustWeather is a simple weather app. View the current weather. Minimum of functions - maximum useful information. Supports Monet, per-app language and predictive gesture back.

  • Lumbear

    Atmospheric clicker game where you have to cut down a part of a tree in one direction or another in time. Break tree, earn money, buy skins that completely change the look of the game. Powered by the Solar2D engine.

  • Gopnation

    Help the gopnik Misha, who wears his red moccasins, bought for him by his father for sixteen years, to eat like more seeds! But you can’t drop seeds - you will get disrespect. Powered by the Solar2D engine.
